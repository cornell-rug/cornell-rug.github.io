Our next R User Group meeting will be on **Tuesday, May 5 at 4pm** in Mann 102 and on Zoom ([link](https://cornell.zoom.us/j/95723147511?pwd=yTARsRFrUPSD3SaHdUAeJiab6obDZI.1)).
 
Scientific results are often text-based and not easily reproduced. [TIB Knowledge Loom](https://knowledgeloom.tib.eu/) is a digital library of analysis-ready code and figures to accompany scientific publications. This meeting will present a demo of the [dtreg](https://gitlab.com/TIBHannover/lki/knowledge-loom/dtreg-r) package and publishing code with this new digital library. 

Snack and drinks will be provided. Hope to see you there!
