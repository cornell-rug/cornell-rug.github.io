Our next R User Group meeting will be on **Tuesday, February 3 at 4pm** in Mann 102 and on Zoom ([link](https://cornell.zoom.us/j/95723147511?pwd=yTARsRFrUPSD3SaHdUAeJiab6obDZI.1)).
 
In this meeting, Ryan Crawford will give an **Introduction to targets for workflow management**. 

The R package targets is a powerful package that makes work safer and faster by keeping track of what tasks have been done and what tasks remain. This talk will introduce the package and show some basic functionality.
